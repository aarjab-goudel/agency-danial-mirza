Privacy Policy

Respecting and safeguarding your privacy is crucial to us. Our Privacy Policy describes how we gather, use, store, and disclose your information when you use our website. The policy applies to all services offered on our site, and we recommend reading it thoroughly to understand our practices.

We collect different types of non-personally identifiable information for various purposes to enhance your experience. We employ technologies such as cookies, web beacons, or unique device identifiers to identify your device and understand your interaction with our service. Additionally, we use Google Analytics to track and report website traffic, helping us understand how visitors interact with our website. We do not collect personal information about our users.

You have the right to reject our use of cookies when you first visit our site. Note, however, that rejecting cookies may affect the functionality of our website. To opt-out from Google Analytics, you may install Google's opt-out browser add-on. Remember, you have control over your data and can request its deletion at any time.

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your non-personally identifiable information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. However, we constantly review and update our practices and security measures to adhere to the industry standards.
